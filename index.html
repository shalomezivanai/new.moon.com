About us

NEW MOON MALL

Description

my name is Shalome Zivanai and I am the co-founder of New Moon. It is with great pleasure to announce that my company will focus on the renovation, rebranding and re-establishment of the development of our country Zimbabwe. I do believe that with the help of the youth, initiatives will be made in order for the country to be recognized on a national and global stage. We want to help build the country in such a way that people from the first world countries will want to invest in our country for the benefit of everyone, both the young and the old. Targeting to create jobs, encourage tourist attraction and bring people from the diaspora back home. New Moon signifies a blank canvas, a clean slate upon which new intentions and goals can be set. It encourages individuals to reflect on their past experiences, release what no longer serves them, and embrace the potential for growth and change. With everything Zimbabweans have gone through it is only right to establish something that the country will take pride in. The New Moon is often associated with introspection, self-reflection, and setting intentions. It is a powerful time for manifesting desires and initiating new projects or ventures.

Generation Z remains the only generation that is innovative, technological, sophisticated and will soon develop everlasting initiatives given the right platforms and opportunities. As the older generation forfeits, it is time for the youth to show strength and courage. New Moon aims to build malls in all parts of Zimbabwe with the help of investors and the citizens of Zimbabwe we hope to achieve this goal. A mall in every city will be beneficial to everyone and help grow the economy.
